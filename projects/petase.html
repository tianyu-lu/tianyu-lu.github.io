Highlights

Generated sequence 18 times the catalytic rate of the wildtype

Curated sequence to kinetics function dataset for hydrolases

Applied novel transfer learning method to PETase engineering

Details

I custom tailored the conditioning algorithm by Brookes et al. for PETase protein function optimization. To mitigate the lack of PETase kinetics data, I applied transfer learning to the oracle predictive function. The larger dataset for the first step of transfer learning was curated from SABIO-RK kinetics database.
